Discover Brownsville

Brownsville is known for its rich cultural heritage and proximity to the Mexican border.

Brownsville boasts a rich cultural diversity with strong Mexican influences, evident in its festivals, food, and traditions. Events like the Charro Days Fiesta, a week-long celebration of Brownsvilleâ€™s binational heritage with its sister city Matamoros, Mexico, showcases traditional dances, parades, and festivities.

Brownsville has become a focal point for space enthusiasts due to the nearby SpaceX launch site at Boca Chica. The site has garnered attention for its role in launching Starship rockets, and visitors may get the chance to witness a rocket launch or explore the surrounding area for a taste of the future of space travel.

Did You Know?

Brownsville is home to the historic Palo Alto Battlefield.

The city hosts the annual Charro Days festival celebrating Mexican-American culture.

Brownsville is considered the Chess Capital of Texas!
